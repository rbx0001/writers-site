1. What Is WRITERS?

WRITERS is a community-owned meme token on Solana that celebrates graffiti and street art culture. It's a token for writers — the people who've been moving in silence, painting the unseen corners of the world, long before crypto existed.

There's no CEO. No VC. No roadmap written by suits. Just a can, a wall, and a blockchain.

2. Why Graffiti + Crypto?

Graffiti and crypto share the same DNA:

  • Permissionless — No one asked if you could paint that wall. No one asked if you could launch a token.
  • Pseudonymous — Writers use tags. Degens use wallets.
  • Immutable — A tag on a wall won't last forever, but a token on Solana might.
  • Anti-establishment — Graffiti rejects the gallery. Crypto rejects the bank.
  • Community — Writers respect writers. Degens respect degens.

$WRTRS is the crossover. The bridge between spray cans and smart contracts.
